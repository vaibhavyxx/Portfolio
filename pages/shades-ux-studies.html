Shades' User Research Internship

This is my process for getting user response and feedback for a social media/news app company.

In September 2024, I had spent the next 12 weeks interviewing college students to gather their feedback about the app. Part of my responsibilities includeded recruiting people for questionnaire, increasing campus pressence for the app and increasing app's retention rate.

By the end of November, I had compiled a presentation to discuss my experience and share feedbacks from testers. As a result, I made this presentation which goes in depth about my findings.
